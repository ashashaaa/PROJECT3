MEMORY AID

Memory aid is a calendar and diary app tailored specifically for people with memory impairments. It is also a great way for people to document their daily tasks in detail. Memory Aid also features a profile system where you can write about your likes and dislikes and write about your relationships. Please allow push notifications so you can use the app to its fullest potential

What makes us special?

In this reality, we are bombarded with responsibilities and huge to-do lists. After documenting your tasks, Memory Aid will send your push notifications asking whether you have completed the app or not. Once completed, you have the option to describe how the task made you feel and challenges and joys you encountered doing them. This function is useful for personal review, and also serves as a great memory aid when you are seeking for medical help.

Plan Ahead

There are so many things you want to do, but most of the time you forget that you even planned it. Nothing task is too silly to mark down. You can colour code your tasks and relationships according to importance and categories.

Reminders and Review

After downloading an app, the biggest mistake is to forget you even downloaded it. With push notifications, Memory Aid will periodically remind you of your upcoming tasks. By reviewing and describing the experience of each task upon completion, it will make therapy sessions much easier and productive.

Your Profile

The profile system is a great way to remind yourself of your likes and dislikes. You can also record detailed profiles of your relationships which also shows the tasks you have completed with them.

No more awkward silences!

Don't you just hate it when your mind goes blank when your therapist asks about your week? By recording in detail the happenings of your week, you can make you sessions much more productive.

Create your visual relationship web!

This app will easily help you create a relationship web which could quickly and efficiently remind you of their importance to you!
